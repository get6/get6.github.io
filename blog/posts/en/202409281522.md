I put together a list of good topics to talk about with strangers.

## First Meeting Topics

These are great topics for breaking the ice when you first meet someone.

1. Quick self-introduction
2. MBTI
3. Birthday
4. Whether you drink/smoke
5. Major and current job
6. Whether you like games or not
7. Complimenting their clothes or accessories
8. Height
9. Whatever it is, compliment the other person

## Travel

These are travel-related topics you can chat about.

1. Preferred travel style (relaxation VS adventure)
2. Domestic travel VS international travel
3. Places you want to visit
4. Places you've been before
5. The best place you've ever visited
6. Most memorable moment from a trip
7. Whether you collect souvenirs from every trip (magnets, postcards, pins, etc.)
8. Whether you can swim
9. Food you want to try while traveling

## Food

These are food-related topics you can chat about. Cooking shows are also a great topic.

1. Favorite food
2. Food you can't eat
3. Food you dislike
4. Dishes you're good at making
5. Dishes you've tried making before
6. Your own special recipe
7. Your go-to restaurant
8. Whether you handle spicy food well
9. Favorite cafe
10. Whether you can drink coffee (or can't)
11. Regular restaurant

## Movies/TV Shows

People usually spend a lot of time watching things. You can find out what they watch on TV, YouTube, Netflix, etc.

1. Favorite movie/show
2. All-time favorite movie/show
3. Memorable scene
4. Favorite genre
5. Most recent movie/show you watched
6. Favorite writer
7. Favorite director
8. Favorite actor/actress
9. An actor/actress you trust to always deliver
10. Action VS Romance

## Music

These are topics you can ask someone who likes music.

1. Favorite song
2. Favorite genre (music taste)
3. Favorite artist
4. Go-to karaoke song
5. Song you've been listening to lately
6. Favorite classical piece
7. Song you're currently obsessed with
8. Whether you like opera/musicals
9. Concerts you've been to
10. Festivals you've attended

## Interests

If you want to find out what someone likes, try asking about these first.

1. Whether you like exercise
2. What your recent hobby is
3. Favorite season
4. Favorite animal (or pets you have)
5. Something you want to learn
6. Things you want to do in the future
7. What makes you the happiest
8. What you do on your days off
9. iPhone VS Galaxy
10. Whether you like amusement parks (rides)
11. Favorite hairstyle

## Question List with Someone

1. Do you have any hobbies (after work, on weekends, after school)?
2. Where did you get your hair cut? I get a down perm every two months because ~
3. Do you like tteokbokki? I find myself craving it at least once a week.
4. Is there anything you want to get better at? I want to get better at programming, so I'm trying bit by bit, but it's not as easy as I thought.
5. What's your ideal type? Kind-hearted? Career-driven?
6. I don't like swearing. Hearing it bothers me too. When someone swears, it really gets to me.
7. Don't you want to go to the beach? It's getting so cold lately, the winter wind must be brutal.
8. I used to play League of Legends a bit when I was younger, but I can't play at all these days.
9. Where did you get your wallet? I only carry cards, so I bought a card case, but it's surprisingly inconvenient.
10. I want to get married before 30. Could you give me one piece of advice?
11. Is there any food you can't eat? I eat everything — carrots, cucumbers, you name it.
12. What time do you need to head home? I'm worried you might be staying late because of me.
13. Is there anything I should fix about myself?
14. Do you invest in stocks? I'm stuck holding SK Hynix right now...
15. I'm stressed about money these days. I want to earn more but I'm still looking for ways.
16. Do you think we'll still be meeting up in 5 years?
17. What's the most exciting thing in your life? For me, it's dating or being in the talking stage. The memories are bittersweet and I have regrets, so it makes me want to do better.
18. I want to live on my own, but I need to save up more money, so I can't just move out. It's a dilemma.
19. What did you do to your shoulders? Why are they so broad? I want shoulders like that.
20. I'm heavily influenced by IT companies. I tend to follow new tech and trends pretty quickly. What I'm learning these days is Flutter, and I'm really excited about it.
21. Do you have a favorite place? Somewhere fun to go together, like a zoo or an amusement park.
22. I've become more emotional as I've gotten older. I cried watching Coco.
23. What songs have you been listening to lately? I like upbeat songs that you'd hear at a club.
24. If you had a lot of money, what would you do? Travel the world? Buy an expensive sports car? Or study abroad in the US?
25. If I started a YouTube channel, do you think I could get famous?
26. You really work hard. They say consistency is a talent — I'm jealous.
27. Do you have a bucket list? I want to try bungee jumping or skydiving.

## KakaoTalk Polaroid Questions

I saw these questions somewhere on KakaoTalk, and the questions were so good that I had to save them. You can learn about people's relationships through these.

1. Who is someone so cute that just thinking about them makes your whole day happy?
2. Who is someone who doesn't hesitate to give you tough love for your own good?
3. Who is someone who surprises you every time you see them because they've changed so much?
4. Who is someone who always shows up before the meeting time to be considerate of you?
5. Who is someone who has qualities you want to learn from?
6. Who is someone you'd still want to take photos with 10 years from now?
7. Who is the person most similar to you?
8. Who is the first person you think of when you're having a hard time?
9. Who is someone who taught you a life lesson?
10. Who is the person who has said "thank you" to you the most?
11. Who is someone who silently and steadfastly believes in you?
12. Who is someone you can't express it in words, but you're always grateful for in your heart?
13. Who is someone you end up sharing your worries with the most?
14. Who is someone who makes you seaweed soup on your birthday every year?
15. Who is someone who showed you warmth through actions rather than words?
16. Who is the person who listens to your stories the most attentively?
17. Who is the person who gives you gifts the most often?
18. Who is someone who generously understood your mistakes?
19. Who is someone you only realized you were grateful for after time had passed?
20. Who is someone who offered you warm comfort during tough times?
